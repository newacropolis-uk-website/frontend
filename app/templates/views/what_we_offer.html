FURHTER INFORMATION

The 16-week course will introduce you to the mayor systems of thought of Eat and West and the principles of Philosophical practice as understood in the Classical Schools of Antiquity. The Theoretical Programme is an introduction to the comparative study of traditions from East & West which are arranged under three subject headings: Ethics, Socipolitics and Philosophy. The Practical Programme looks at fundamental philosophical questions under the headings: Know Thyself, Harmony with Others, Transformation, Overcoming Crises and Being Happy.

ETHICS

- Understanding others

Introduction to ethics and the major concepts of the philosophies of India, Tibet, Ancient Egypt and Neoplatonism.

SOCIPOLITICS

- Living in harmony with others

Introduction to Socipolitics and the major concepts of the Confucius, Plato and the Stoics.

PHILOSOPHY OF HISTORY

- Being part of something greater

Introduction to Philosophy of history, the Microcosm and Macrocosom and the Cosmovision of Traditional Societies.
